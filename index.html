Dress Code

Formal & Comfortable

Dress as formal - the most important thing is to feel comfortable! For dresses it can be short or long, any color you like (except no white!). It is still quite warm in October, between 20-25 degrees during the day.

Vístete de manera formal, ¡lo más importante es sentirte cómoda! Para los vestidos puede ser corto o largo, cualquier color que te guste (¡excepto el blanco!). Todavía hace bastante calor en octubre, entre 20-25 grados durante el día.

Häätyyli on juhlava - tärkeintä että itsellä on mukava olla! Mekko voi olla pitkä tai lyhyt, ja väri on vapaavalintainen(paitsi ei valkoista!). Tumma puku (värikkäämpikin versio) käy hyvin. Lokakuussa lämpötila on päiväsaikaan noin 20-25 astetta.

Visit Murcia

Things to see in Murcia

  • Activities
  • Sightseeing

Explore the Region

There's a lot to see around the region: Visit the historical Cartagena or smaller towns like Caravaca de la Cruz, Cabo de Palos by the coast, or Lorca with its castles. Also, if you're interested in seeing other regions, Alicante has a lot to offer.

The Royal Casino of Murcia

A wonderful building in the heart of Trapería, in which you enter into a building full of art and and exquisite architecture.

Gastronomy

Murcia's gastronomy is one of it's great attractions. Try out caballitos (our favourite appeticer!), Murcian salad or zarangollo. There are plenty of options!

Sanctuary of la Fuensanta

From the Sanctuary you'll see the panoramic view over the city. Do a short hike in the surroundings and finish with an appeticer in the local restaurant.

Wine & Beer tasting

Try out wine tasting in the famous wine regions like Jumilla, Yecla, or Bullas. Estrella de Levante is Murcia's famous beer brand - the factory also organizes tours located close to the city center.

Murcia Old Town & the Cathedral

Stroll around the narrow streets of the Old Town, see the Cathedral Square, Romea Square and Glorieta Garden.
